Mobile Header (≤768px):

- Form inputs: 48-52px height

- Body text: 16px minimum (14px acceptable for secondary)

- Line height: 1.5-1.6 for readability

Spacing:
- Mobile: 12px, 16px, 24px
- Desktop: 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px

Layouts:
- Desktop: Multi-column grids
- Tablet: 2-column
- Mobile: Single column (stacked)

Forms:
- Labels: Above inputs (not inline)
- Inputs: Full width, ≥48px height
- Buttons: Full width or min 50% width
- Submit: Sticky at bottom or prominent
