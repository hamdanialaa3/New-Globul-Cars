# القسم 12: المتطلبات القانونية البلغارية المُفصّلة

## 12.1 Private Sellers

```
Legal Limits:
  • ≤5 cars/year: considered private
  • >5 cars/year: must register as trader

Requirements:
  • Valid ID
  • Ownership proof (registration certificate)
  • No VAT requirement
  • No business license needed

Obligations:
  • Honest description
  • Declare known defects
  • Valid technical inspection (if >3 years old)
  • Ownership transfer at traffic police
```

## 12.2 Car Dealers

```
Registration Requirements:
  1. Business Registration:
     • Търговски регистър (Commercial Register)
     • EIK/BULSTAT number
     • Company type: ET, OOD, EOOD, AD
  
  2. Tax Registration:
     • NRA (National Revenue Agency)
     • VAT: mandatory if revenue >BGN 50,000/year
     • Corporate tax: 10%
  
  3. Licenses:
     • Trade license (depending on municipality)
     • Environmental permits (if applicable)
  
  4. Insurance:
     • Liability insurance
     • Professional indemnity
     • Showroom insurance

Documents to Upload:
  - Certificate of Registration (Удостоверение за актуално състояние)
  - EIK/BULSTAT certificate
  - VAT registration (if applicable)
  - Business address proof
  - Owner ID card/passport
  - Insurance policy
  - Bank account statement

Compliance:
  • Consumer Protection Act (Закон за защита на потребителите)
  • 14-day return right for consumers
  • Warranty obligations
  • Price transparency (all costs disclosed)
  • Distance selling regulations
```

## 12.3 Companies

```
Registration Requirements:
  1. Full Company Registration:
     • Commercial Register entry
     • EIK/BULSTAT
     • Company statutes
     • Shareholder structure
     • Board of Directors
  
  2. Tax & VAT:
     • VAT registration (mandatory for dealers)
     • Corporate tax registration
     • Quarterly tax filings
     • Annual financial statements
  
  3. Data Protection:
     • GDPR Compliance
     • DPO (Data Protection Officer) if >250 employees
     • Privacy policy
     • Cookie consent
  
  4. Labor:
     • Employment contracts
     • Social security registration
     • Occupational health & safety

Documents to Upload:
  - Company registration certificate
  - Company statutes (Устав)
  - Board decision (Решение на управителя)
  - VAT certificate
  - Tax registration
  - Bank account (corporate)
  - Authorized signatory documents
  - Insurance policies (comprehensive)
  - Data Processing Agreement (if handling customer data)

Verification Process:
  1. Online application
  2. Document submission
  3. EIK verification (API: https://portal.registryagency.bg/)
  4. VAT verification (API: NRA)
  5. Legal review (2-5 days)
  6. On-site visit (for fleets >100)
  7. Contract negotiation
  8. Final approval
  9. Onboarding

Duration: 1-2 weeks
```
