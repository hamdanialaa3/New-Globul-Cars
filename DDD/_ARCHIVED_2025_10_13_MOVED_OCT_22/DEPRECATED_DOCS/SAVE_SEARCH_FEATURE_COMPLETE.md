## 🎨 **How It Works:**

### **Step-by-Step:**

```
1. User goes to: /advanced-search
2. Selects filters:
   - Make: BMW
   - Model: 3 Series
   - Price: €15,000 - €25,000
   - Year: 2020+
   - Location: Sofia
3. Clicks: 💾 "Запази търсенето" (green button)
4. Modal appears
5. User enters name: "BMW 3 Series 2020+ София"
6. Sees summary: "BMW 3 Series · 2020-2024 · €15,000-€25,000 · 📍 София"
7. Clicks: "Запази"
8. Toast: "Search saved successfully!"
9. Goes to: /saved-searches
10. Sees the saved search!
11. Clicks "Search" to reapply all filters
```
